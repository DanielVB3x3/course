Technology, Society and Nature.

    Welcome to this virtual space dedicated to debate and analize the inteactions between Technology, Society and Nature.



        The correlations between these three areas deserve greater attention from us.
        Technologies are developed in certain societies only if some type of value is recognized in them and once created and assimilated they can affect the very structure and culture of that same society as well as the way in which societies perceive and interact with the environment and resources natural.
         Considering the positive and negative potentialities inherent to the technologies at our disposal, the changes in the hegemonic economic system, the current environmental crisis and much more, this initiative was made.
